Courses

Available Courses

  • Computer Engineering

    Duration: 4 years

    Overview: This course covers various aspects of computer science and engineering, including programming, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems.

  • Information Technology

    Duration: 4 years

    Overview: Information Technology focuses on the application of computing technology to solve real-world problems. It includes topics like software development, database management, and networking.

  • Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ENTC)

    Duration: 4 years

    Overview: ENTC combines principles of electronics and telecommunications to design and develop communication systems, electronic devices, and integrated circuits.
